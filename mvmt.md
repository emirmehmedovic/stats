# Movement types / tip operacije (vizuelna podjela)

## Scheduled (R) / Redovni
- Linijski putnički (timetable)
- Linijski cargo (timetable)
- Code-share varijante (marketing/operating)

## Non-scheduled komercijalni (Charter / Air taxi / Ad-hoc)
- Charter putnički (ad-hoc)
- Charter serije (povremene rotacije)
- Air taxi (komercijalni ad-hoc, često manji avion)
- Ad-hoc cargo (charter cargo)

## Cargo (svrha leta)
- Scheduled cargo (ako je po redu letenja)
- Non-scheduled cargo (ad-hoc/charter)
- Cargo sa dangerous goods (DG)

## General Aviation (GA)
- GA – Business flight
- GA – Private flight (own-use)
- Aero-klub / sports aviation

## Humanitarian (civilno)
- Humanitarian flight

## Ferry / positioning
- Ferry / delivery / maintenance reposition
- Positioning (prazan let)

## Medicinske i hitne operacije
- Hospital / MEDEVAC
- SAR (search and rescue)
- Emergency landing
- Diversion (preusmjerenje)

## Trening / školske operacije
- Training: Touch and go
- Training: Approach
- Ostali školski letovi

## All other (operativno/tehnički)
- Technical flight / technical landing
- Helicopter operations

## VFR civilni
- Civilian VFR (standard)
- VFR iznad FL100 (posebni slučajevi)

## State / diplomatic / military
- State aircraft (diplomatic)
- Military
